    <h1>Chocolate Chip Cookies</h1>
    <p>This is a simple and delicious recipe for homemade chocolate chip cookies that everyone will love!</p>
    
    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>1 cup butter, softened</li>
      <li>1 cup white sugar</li>
      <li>2 cups all-purpose flour</li>
      <li>1 cup chocolate chips</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h2>Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).</li>
      <li>Cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.</li>
      <li>Stir in flour until combined, then fold in chocolate chips.</li>
      <li>Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.</li>
    </ol>

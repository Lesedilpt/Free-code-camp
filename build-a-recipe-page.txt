    <h1>Chocolate Mug Cake</h1>

    <p>This quick and easy chocolate mug cake is perfect when you're craving dessert but don't want to bake an entire cake. It's rich, moist, and ready in under 5 minutes!</p>

    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>4 tablespoons all-purpose flour</li>
      <li>2 tablespoons cocoa powder</li>
      <li>2 tablespoons sugar</li>
      <li>3 tablespoons milk</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>In a microwave-safe mug, mix flour, cocoa powder, and sugar.</li>
      <li>Add milk and stir until smooth.</li>
      <li>Microwave on high for 1 minute and 30 seconds.</li>
      <li>Let it cool slightly, then enjoy warm!</li>
    </ol>
